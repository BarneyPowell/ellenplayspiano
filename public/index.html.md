# Ellen Plays Piano homepage

> Ellen Plays Piano is Ellen Dean’s private piano tuition site for children and adults in London N14, Enfield, nearby North London and online.

## Overview

Ellen Plays Piano offers classical piano lessons from a calm home studio in London N14. Lessons are rooted in thirty years of musical experience, patient guidance and the joy of discovery. Ellen teaches pupils from Enfield, Southgate, Winchmore Hill, Palmers Green, Oakwood and nearby North London, with remote lessons available where appropriate.

## Teacher

Ellen Dean is a piano teacher whose work is grounded in the classical tradition: careful technique, structured progression and respect for the discipline that makes real musicianship possible. She prepares pupils for ABRSM and Trinity examinations, but exams are only part of the teaching approach.

Ellen’s broader aim is to help each pupil, whether seven or seventy, develop a lasting personal relationship with music.

## Lesson types

### Children aged 7–11: Building Foundations

Lessons for younger children focus on developing technique, communication and imagination. Pupils can work towards graded examinations, with lessons paced around the child in front of Ellen.

Typical duration: 30 minutes.

### Ages 11–16: Young Musicians

Lessons for older children and teenagers expand repertoire and deepen interpretation. Pupils start to appreciate the rewards of serious, dedicated practice. Lessons take place on Ellen’s Kemble upright piano at her home studio in London N14, close to Enfield and Southgate.

Typical duration: 45 minutes.

### Adults: A Fresh Start — or a Return

Adult lessons are shaped around the pupil’s pace, goals and music, whether they are starting for the first time or rediscovering piano after years away. Remote piano tuition is available where it suits the pupil.

Typical duration: 45–60 minutes.

## Teaching philosophy

- Patience over pressure: real progress comes from consistent, calm practice rather than rushing.
- Structure gives freedom: a solid technical foundation enables creativity.
- The whole person: piano can teach focus, resilience and the satisfaction of craft.
- Beauty matters: Ellen chooses repertoire with care, even at the earliest stages.

## Practical details

- Price: £40 per hour, pro-rated for shorter sessions.
- Enhanced DBS checked.
- 30 years’ experience.
- ABRSM and Trinity preparation available.
- Location: London N14, near Enfield.
- Online/remote lessons available where suitable.
